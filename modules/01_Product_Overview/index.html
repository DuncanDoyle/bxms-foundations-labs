Case Management

The RHPAM engine "choreographs" a sequence of automated activities, involves humans by exception only.

RHPAM notifies humans about which action to take next, in a set, preordered manner.

RHPAM suggests possible next steps. Humans decide the next best action, eventually using an ad hoc activity to override. Users look at RHPAM as a task and document organizer.
