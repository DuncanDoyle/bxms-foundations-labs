Case Management

The RHPAM engine "choreographs" a sequence of automated activities, it involves humans by exception.

The RHPAM says to humans which is the next action in a quite pre-ordered manner.

The RHPAM suggests possible next steps, Humans decide the next best action eventually overriding with an ad hoc activity. Users look at RHPAM as a task and document organizer.
