Business process monitoring involves real-time scrutiny of an activity or set of activities to accomplish a specific organizational goal.

Process monitoring enables an organization to measure and analyze process performance and make decisions that improve the speed, quality, and efficiency of business processes.

As part of Process Automation Manager, process monitoring gives visibility into processes, process instances, and user tasks.

User Persona: Patrick

  • Process administrator at large organization

  • Cross-functional collaboration part of day-to-day work

    • Works closely with process manager, IT developer, system admin

  • Manages and tracks status of tens of thousands of process instances

  • Routinely filters large data sets based on specific criteria to find subset of instances

    • Uses filtering to isolate specific instances and identify problems
