Business process monitoring is a real-time scrutiny of an activity or set of activities to accomplish a specific organizational goal

It enables an organization to measure and analyze process performance, make decisions that will improve the speed, quality and efficiency of business processes

As part of business process management, gain visibility into processes, process instances and user tasks

user persona

Patrick - Process Administrator

  • Patrick is a Process Admin at a large organization.
  • Cross-functional collaboration is part of his day-to-day work. He works closely with Process manager, IT developer and System admin.
  • He manages tens of thousands of process instances and track their status.
  • He routinely filters large datasets, based on specific criteria, to find a particular subset of instances.

  • Through filtering datasets, he expects to isolate specific instances and identify problems.

 

....and make the organization happy with quick access to data

Monitoring sla

Track SLAs to improve process performance

Whenever SLA due date is set, process/node instance will be annotated with additional information:

  • calculated due date
  • SLA compliance level
    • N/A - when there is no SLA due date 
    • Pending - when instance is active with due date set 
    • Met - when instance was completed before SLA due date 
    • Violated - when instance was not completed before SLA due date 
    • Aborted - when instance was aborted before SLA due date
